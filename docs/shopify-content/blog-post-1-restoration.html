If you've ever bought a used LEGO set online, you know the gamble. Missing pieces, yellowed bricks, mystery smells — it's a minefield. That's exactly why we do things differently at Hadley Bricks.

Every single used set we sell goes through a full restoration process before it reaches you. Here's exactly what that looks like.

Step 1: Sourcing

We source our used LEGO from a mix of charity shops, car boot sales, private collections, and bulk lots. We're selective — we don't buy anything that looks like it's been through a cement mixer. But even clean-looking sets need proper work before they're ready to sell.

Step 2: Deep Clean

Every brick goes through a thorough cleaning process. We soak parts in warm soapy water, scrub away dirt and grime, and dry everything carefully. Yellowed white and light grey pieces get special treatment where possible. The goal is bricks that look and feel like you'd want them to.

Step 3: Sort & Inventory

This is the time-consuming part. We sort every piece by type, colour, and size, then cross-reference against the official inventory for that set. We use BrickLink's comprehensive parts database to make sure we're not missing anything — right down to the smallest 1x1 plate.

Step 4: Completeness Check

Once sorted, we verify the set is complete. If any pieces are missing, we source exact replacements from BrickLink or our own spare parts inventory. We don't use substitutes or "close enough" parts. If a set calls for a dark red 2x4 brick, that's exactly what goes in the box.

Step 5: Instructions & Packaging

We include original instructions where available. If the original manual is missing or damaged, we source a replacement or provide a link to LEGO's official digital instructions. Sets are packaged in resealable bags, sorted by build stage where practical, and shipped in sturdy boxes with proper protection.

Why Buy Restored?

Restored LEGO sets offer brilliant value. You get a complete, clean set — often a retired set you can't buy new anymore — at a fraction of the sealed price. And because we've done all the hard work of sourcing, cleaning, and verifying, you can build with confidence as soon as it arrives.

Every restored set from Hadley Bricks comes with our completeness guarantee. If anything is missing, we'll sort it.
